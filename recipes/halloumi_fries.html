Halloumi Fries

List of Ingredients

  • Cilantro (1 bag)
  • White jokes and herb (4g)
  • Cabbage mix (150 g)
  • Mayonnaise (50 g)
  • Asian dressing (20 g)
  • Chili (1/2)
  • Lime (1)
  • Peanuts (20 g)
  • Halloumi (200 g)
  • Carrot (1)
  • Spring Onions (1)

    Instructions

  1. Coarsely grate carrot (unpeeled). Coarsely grate the halloumi. Finely chop spring onions. Halve chillies lengthwise, remove seeds and chop finely.
  2. Beat eggs* and White Leeks & Herbs in a bowl. Add carrot, halloumi, spring onion and chilli and stir. Add wheat flour* and a large pinch of pepper*, and mix it all together.
  3. Heat a little olive oil* in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Shape the stuffing into fritters (2 per person) and fry for 5-6 minutes on each side. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels.
  4. Cut the lime into wedges. Coarsely chop the peanuts. Mix a little olive oil* and a squeeze of lime juice in a bowl, and season with a pinch of salt* and pepper*. Add cabbage mixture and peanuts and toss together.
  5. Mix Asian dressing and mayonnaise in a small bowl. Coarsely chop coriander.
  6. Arrange fritters and coleslaw on plates. Top with cilantro. Serve with Asian mayonnaise and lime wedges.
